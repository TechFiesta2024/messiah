Building a Maze
Delve into the fascinating world of robotics as we explore how to build a maze solver. Explore the circuit diagram and code explanation, understanding the hardware and software components required for the maze-solving robot.

Building a Race Car
Dive into the realm of race car robotics as we explore how to build a high-performance racing vehicle. Explore the circuit diagram and code explanation, unraveling the intricacies of building a race car from scratch. Conduct a cost-to-performance analysis, evaluating the trade-offs between different components and features to optimize the race car's performance within budget constraints. Discuss control schemes for race cars, including manual control, remote control, and autonomous control using sensors and onboard processing.
We encourage you to actively participate, ask questions, and collaborate with your fellow participants. By the end of the workshop, you'll be equipped with the knowledge and skills to embark on your own robotics projects with confidence.
