Basic Circuits
Dive into the world of digital logic gates and understand their role in processing binary data. Understand the principles behind binary addition and subtraction, and explore the construction of half adders, full adders, and subtractors. Understand how MUX circuits work to route data inputs to selected outputs based on control signals.
We encourage you to actively participate, ask questions, and collaborate with your fellow participants. By the end of the workshop, you'll be equipped with the knowledge and skills to design and analyze basic digital circuits with confidence.

Once again, welcome to the Basic Circuits workshop! We're excited to embark on this journey of exploration and learning with you.
